An evening of jazz

Step out of the ordinary and into the rhythm of the age! Every Monday, Thursday, and Saturday night, the doors swing open and the band strikes up the hottest jazz in town. Bright horns sing, the piano rolls like thunder, and the drums keep a heartbeat that stirs the soul.

About us

We’re a small team with experience in New Orleans kitchens, bakeries, and bars.

  • Simone Gautier, General Manager — has managed family-owned restaurants in the French Quarter for over a decade.
  • Marcus Turner Head Chef — grew up in New Orleans and started working in restaurant kitchens.
  • Lena Irwin, Pastry Chef — learned baking from her mother and has worked in cafés across the city.
  • Andre Étienne, Sous Chef — spent years fishing for restaurants along the Gulf Coast.
  • Theo Harlow, Bartender — enjoys classic cocktails and talking with guests at the end of a long day.

Together, they focus on simple hospitality, solid cooking, and creating a place people want to come back to.
