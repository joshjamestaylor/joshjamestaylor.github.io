New Automobile Unveiled

February 14, 1924

Miss Darcy, our benefactor and patron of the arts, has recently purchased a new automobile. With the license plate "D41924", it is said to be the talk of the town. The car will be unveiled at the upcoming Gala night, promising an unforgettable evening of music and style. Her reservations here at the Golden Age Society are not few and far between, and we are honored to have her as a guest.
